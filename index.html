My passions

Whenever someone asks me what my favorite video game is, I usually never say "DDR", but that's because it's SO much more to me than just a game. Not only is it amazing and fun cardio, but the competitive aspect of the game has produced many of my best friendships and going to tournaments is like a family reunion.
I'm so glad that I decided to start playing close to 20 years ago, continued to keep after my local scene died out, and joined the national scene 10 years ago; I couldn't imagine my life without this game and the wonderful people I've met along the way. I used to be one of the best players in the world and I'm slowly coming out of retirement; I hope to be a top level player again one day.

Beyond DDR, at the beginning of this year I decided to start pursuing a dream of mine that I've had for a while now: comedy. It started with taking improv classes at The Annoyance in Chicago, but I also started taking an acting class as well. While pursuing those dreams of stardom have been put on hold for this bootcamp, I am in anticipation of when I can start again; I desire to be on the big screen one day and I would at the very least like to move closer to the city.
